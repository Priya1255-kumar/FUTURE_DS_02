# FUTURE_DS_02
CUSTOMER RETENTION AND CHURN ANALYSIS 

Introduction
This dashboard analyzes customer churn in a telecom company. The main goal is to understand why customers are leaving and identify key factors that affect churn. The analysis focuses on contract type, internet service, tenure, monthly charges, and payment method.

Results
Total Customers: 249
Churned Customers: 47
Churn Rate: 19%
Key findings:
Customers with month-to-month contracts have higher churn.
Customers with longer contracts (1 year and 2 years) have lower churn.
New customers (low tenure) are more likely to leave.
Customers using electronic check payment show higher churn.
Fiber optic internet users have slightly higher churn compared to others.

Conclusion
The analysis shows that contract type, tenure, and payment method strongly affect customer churn.
To reduce churn, the company should:
Encourage long-term contracts
Focus on retaining new customers
Promote automatic payment methods
Improve service experience for high-risk groups
This dashboard helps management quickly identify problem areas and make better business decisions.
